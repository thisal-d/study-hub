### **Solution to Question 1: FCFS Scheduling**

**Gantt Chart:**
Since the processes arrive at time $0$ in the order $P_1, P_2, P_3$, they execute sequentially based on their arrival.

`| P1 (0-8) | P2 (8-12) | P3 (12-13) |`

**Calculations:**

1.  **Waiting Time (WT) = Start Time - Arrival Time**
    *   $P_1$: Starts at 0. WT = $0 - 0 = 0$ ms.
    *   $P_2$: Starts at 8 (after $P_1$ finishes). WT = $8 - 0 = 8$ ms.
    *   $P_3$: Starts at 12 (after $P_2$ finishes). WT = $12 - 0 = 12$ ms.

2.  **Turnaround Time (TAT) = Completion Time - Arrival Time**
    *   $P_1$: Completes at 8. TAT = $8 - 0 = 8$ ms.
    *   $P_2$: Completes at 12. TAT = $12 - 0 = 12$ ms.
    *   $P_3$: Completes at 13. TAT = $13 - 0 = 13$ ms.

**Filled Table:**

| Process | Burst Time (ms) | Waiting Time (ms) | Turnaround Time (ms) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| $P_{1}$ | 8 | 0 | 8 |
| $P_{2}$ | 4 | 8 | 12 |
| $P_{3}$ | 1 | 12 | 13 |
| **Average** | **-** | **6.67 ms** | **11 ms** |

*(Note: $20/3 = 6.67$ ms and $33/3 = 11$ ms)*
