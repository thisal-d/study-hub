### **Solution to Practice Question 2: Round Robin**

**How to Solve:**
1.  Execute each process for the time quantum ($q=3$).
2.  If the burst is less than $q$, the process finishes early and the next one starts immediately.
3.  Calculate Waiting Time as $Waiting = Turnaround - Burst$.

**Gantt Chart:**
`| P1 (0-3) | P2 (3-6) | P3 (6-9) | P4 (9-10) | P1 (10-12) | P2 (12-15) | P2 (15-16) |`

**Calculations:**

| Process | Burst Time (ms) | Waiting Time (ms) | Turnaround Time (ms) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| $P_{1}$ | 5 | 7 | 12 |
| $P_{2}$ | 7 | 9 | 16 |
| $P_{3}$ | 3 | 6 | 9 |
| $P_{4}$ | 1 | 9 | 10 |
| **Average** | **-** | **7.75 ms** | **11.75 ms** |
