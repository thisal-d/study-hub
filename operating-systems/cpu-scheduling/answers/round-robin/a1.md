### **Solution to Practice Question 1: Round Robin**

**How to Solve:**
1.  **Queue Management**: Maintain a ready queue of processes.
2.  **Time Quantum**: Each process executes for at most $q$ ms. If it doesn't finish, it goes to the back of the queue.
3.  **Completion**: Track when each process finishes to calculate Turnaround Time (Completion - Arrival).

**Gantt Chart:**
Quantum ($q$) = 4 ms. All at $t=0$.
`| P1 (0-4) | P2 (4-8) | P3 (8-12) | P1 (12-16) | P2 (16-18) | P1 (18-22) |`

**Calculations:**

| Process | Burst Time (ms) | Waiting Time (ms) | Turnaround Time (ms) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| $P_{1}$ | 12 | 10 | 22 |
| $P_{2}$ | 6 | 12 | 18 |
| $P_{3}$ | 4 | 8 | 12 |
| **Average** | **-** | **10.00 ms** | **17.33 ms** |
