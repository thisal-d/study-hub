### **Solution to Practice Question 4: Preemptive SJF (SRTF)**

**How to Solve:**
1.  **Continuous Comparison**: Even if $P_3$ arrives at $t=2$, it doesn't preempt $P_2$ because $P_2$ only has 1ms left whereas $P_3$ needs 5ms.
2.  **Sequential Preemption**: $P_1$ handles being preempted by $P_2$ and then having to wait until $P_4$ and $P_3$ also finish.

**Gantt Chart:**
`| P1 (0-1) | P2 (1-3) | P4 (3-4) | P3 (4-9) | P1 (9-15) |`

**Calculations:**

| Process | Arrival Time (ms) | Burst Time (ms) | Waiting Time (ms) | Turnaround Time (ms) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| $P_{1}$ | 0 | 7 | 8 | 15 |
| $P_{2}$ | 1 | 2 | 0 | 2 |
| $P_{3}$ | 2 | 5 | 2 | 7 |
| $P_{4}$ | 3 | 1 | 0 | 1 |
| **Average** | **-** | **-** | **2.50 ms** | **6.25 ms** |
