### **Solution to Practice Question 3: Preemptive SJF (SRTF)**

**How to Solve:**
1.  **Rapid Preemption**: In this scenario, $P_2, P_3, P_4$ are all very short compared to $P_1$.
2.  **Tie-Breaking**: If a new process arrives with a burst equal to the remaining time of the current process (like at $t=3$), you can continue the current process to minimize context switching.

**Gantt Chart:**
`| P1 (0-1) | P2 (1-2) | P3 (2-4) | P4 (4-5) | P5 (5-8) | P1 (8-17) |`
