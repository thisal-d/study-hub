### **Solution to Practice Question 5: Preemptive SJF (SRTF)**

**How to Solve:**
1.  **Multiple Ties**: At $t=2$, $P_1$ remaining is 4 and $P_2$ burst is 4. No preemption.
2.  **Late Arrivals**: $P_4$ and $P_5$ arrive while $P_3$ and $P_2$ are waiting. Always re-evaluate at every completion.

**Gantt Chart:**
`| P1 (0-6) | P3 (6-8) | P4 (8-9) | P5 (9-12) | P2 (12-16) |`
