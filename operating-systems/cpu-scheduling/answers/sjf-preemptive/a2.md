### **Solution to Practice Question 2: Preemptive SJF (SRTF)**

**How to Solve:**
1.  **Monitor Arrivals**: At $t=2$, $P_2$ arrives but $P_1$ has only 1ms left. $P_1$ is shorter so it continues.
2.  **Preemption**: At $t=4$, $P_3$ arrives with burst 1. $P_2$ has 5ms left. $P_3$ preempts $P_2$.

**Gantt Chart:**
`| P1 (0-3) | P2 (3-4) | P3 (4-5) | P4 (5-9) | P2 (9-14) |`

**Calculations:**

| Process | Arrival Time (ms) | Burst Time (ms) | Waiting Time (ms) | Turnaround Time (ms) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| $P_{1}$ | 0 | 3 | 0 | 3 |
| $P_{2}$ | 2 | 6 | 6 | 12 |
| $P_{3}$ | 4 | 1 | 0 | 1 |
| $P_{4}$ | 5 | 4 | 0 | 4 |
| **Average** | **-** | **-** | **1.50 ms** | **5.00 ms** |
