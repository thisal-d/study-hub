### **Solution to Practice Question 1: Non-Preemptive SJF**

**Gantt Chart:**
Since all processes arrive at time $0$, SJF selects the process with the smallest burst time first.
Order: $P_3 (2) \to P_4 (4) \to P_1 (6) \to P_2 (10)$.

`| P3 (0-2) | P4 (2-6) | P1 (6-12) | P2 (12-22) |`

**Calculations:**

1.  **Waiting Time (WT):**
    *   $P_3$: Starts at 0. WT = $0$ ms.
    *   $P_4$: Starts after $P_3$ ($0+2$). WT = $2$ ms.
    *   $P_1$: Starts after $P_4$ ($2+4$). WT = $6$ ms.
    *   $P_2$: Starts after $P_1$ ($6+6$). WT = $12$ ms.

2.  **Turnaround Time (TAT) = WT + Burst Time:**
    *   $P_3$: $0 + 2 = 2$ ms.
    *   $P_4$: $2 + 4 = 6$ ms.
    *   $P_1$: $6 + 6 = 12$ ms.
    *   $P_2$: $12 + 10 = 22$ ms.

**Filled Table:**

| Process | Burst Time (ms) | Waiting Time (ms) | Turnaround Time (ms) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| $P_{3}$ | 2 | 0 | 2 |
| $P_{4}$ | 4 | 2 | 6 |
| $P_{1}$ | 6 | 6 | 12 |
| $P_{2}$ | 10 | 12 | 22 |
| **Average** | **-** | **5 ms** | **10.5 ms** |
