### **Solution to Practice Question 1: Priority Scheduling**

**Gantt Chart:**
The processes are scheduled based on priority (Smallest Number = Highest Priority).
Order: $P_2 (1) \to P_4 (2) \to P_1 (3) \to P_3 (4)$.

`| P2 (0-1) | P4 (1-6) | P1 (6-16) | P3 (16-18) |`

**Calculations:**

| Process | Burst Time (ms) | Priority | Waiting Time (ms) | Turnaround Time (ms) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| $P_{2}$ | 1 | 1 | 0 | 1 |
| $P_{4}$ | 5 | 2 | 1 | 6 |
| $P_{1}$ | 10 | 3 | 6 | 16 |
| $P_{3}$ | 2 | 4 | 16 | 18 |
| **Average** | **-** | **-** | **5.75 ms** | **10.25 ms** |
